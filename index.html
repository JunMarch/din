Supporting the clever but needy Students

As a way to support these brilliant but needy students, we provide them educational assets like books, pencils, pens etc. to help them study. Most Children lack the privillege to attend class and participate in academics due to the lack of these amenities. Daniel Impact Network has made it upon themselves and thier cherised donors to provide these assests to support them

Each Year, with the aid of our cherised donors and volunteers, we reach out to these children and the less privillege to supply them with the donations we recieve.
